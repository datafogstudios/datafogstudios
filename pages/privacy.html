Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide you with the best learning experience. The types of information we may collect include:

Information You Provide Voluntarily

  • Contact Information: Name and email address when you contact us
  • Learning Progress: Course progress stored locally in your browser
  • Certificate Purchases: Payment information processed securely through Stripe

Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, features used
  • IP Address: For security and analytics purposes

Important: We do not require account creation. Most learning data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage.

How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our educational platform
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • To process certificate purchases securely
  • To improve our courses and user experience
  • To send important updates about our services (only if you opt-in)
  • To detect and prevent fraud or security issues

Cookies & Analytics

We use minimal cookies to enhance your experience:

Essential Cookies

Required for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled:

  • Session management
  • Security features
  • Basic functionality

Analytics Cookies (Optional)

Help us understand how you use our platform:

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Popular courses and features
  • Performance metrics

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Secure Storage: Data stored on secure servers with restricted access
  • Payment Security: We use Stripe for payment processing; we never store credit card information
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and updates
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis

Note: While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

Stripe

For secure payment processing. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Cloudflare

For content delivery and DDoS protection. Subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Formspree

For contact form submissions. Subject to Formspree's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation on how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at howardteam54@gmail.com

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Sending an email notification (if you've opted in)

Continued use of our platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
